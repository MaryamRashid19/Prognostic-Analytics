Dr. Athar Kharal

Assistant Professor at CASPAM, Bahauddin Zakariya University

Dr. Athar Kharal is an Assistant Professor at CASPAM, Bahauddin Zakariya University, where he teaches and conducts research in mathematics and data science. With a PhD in Mathematics, his work focuses on practical applications of mathematical concepts, including data analysis, decision-making systems, and computational methods.

An enthusiastic educator, He teaches courses ranging from advanced mathematics to programming with Python and R. His teaching approach combines theory with hands-on applications, helping students develop both conceptual understanding and technical skills.

Beyond his academic work, He is an avid reader with wide-ranging interests. He regularly explores works across different fields - from science and philosophy to literature and history. This intellectual curiosity informs his teaching and research, allowing him to draw connections between diverse subjects.

Dr. Athar maintains an active presence in academic circles, sharing knowledge through publications and online platforms. His commitment to making complex ideas accessible demonstrates his dedication to both education and research.

I would like to express my deepest gratitude to Dr. Athar for his invaluable guidance, constant encouragement, and expert advice throughout the development of this project. His valuable feedback and thoughtful guidance were crucial in refining the focus and enhancing the quality of this research.

- Maryam Rashid
